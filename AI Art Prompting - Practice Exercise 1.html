Part 1: The Anatomy of a Great Prompt

Read the following text carefully. It will be the basis for the exercises in Part 2.

Creating stunning images with AI art generators like Midjourney or DALL-E is less about magic and more about clear communication. The quality of your output is directly tied to the quality of your inputâ€”your prompt. A well-crafted prompt acts as a detailed blueprint for the AI. Let's break down the essential components.

1. The Subject: This is the 'what' of your image. It's the main noun, the character, or the object you want to see. Start with a clear and simple subject. Instead of just "dog," be specific: "a fluffy Samoyed puppy." Specificity is your best friend.

2. The Style & Medium: This is the 'how.' It defines the artistic look and feel. Do you want a photo or a painting? If a painting, what kind? Examples include: photorealistic, oil painting, watercolor sketch, digital art, anime style, 3D render, Impressionism. This component dramatically changes the outcome.

3. Composition & Framing: This component directs the 'camera.' It tells the AI how to frame the subject. Is it a close-up, a full-body shot, or a wide landscape? Key terms include: portrait, close-up shot, full shot, side view, top-down shot, wide angle shot. This gives you control over the perspective.

4. Lighting: Lighting creates mood. It can transform a simple scene into something dramatic or serene. Be descriptive. Use terms like: cinematic lighting, soft morning light, neon glow, dramatic backlighting, moody shadows.

5. Color Palette: While the AI often chooses colors well, you can guide it for a specific aesthetic. You can specify a mood or list the colors directly. Examples: warm color palette, pastel colors, monochromatic blue, vibrant and saturated colors.

Putting It All Together (An Example):
- Basic Idea: "A robot in a forest."
- Layered Prompt: "A photorealistic full shot of a friendly, vintage robot walking through a misty redwood forest, soft morning light filtering through the canopy, warm color palette of greens and browns."
Each added layer gives the AI more precise instructions, moving from a vague idea to a specific, evocative scene.

Part 2: Practice Questions

Based on your understanding of the text, answer the following questions.

  1. Identify the Components: Read the prompt below and identify its Subject and Lighting component.
    Prompt: "A close-up portrait of an old wizard reading a book, moody shadows cast from a single candle."
    Hint: The 'Subject' is the main character or object of the image. The 'Lighting' describes the source and feel of the light.
  2. Explain the Purpose: Why is it important to specify a 'Style & Medium' in a prompt?
    Hint: Think about the difference between telling an artist to "draw a house" versus "paint a watercolor of a house." What does the extra information provide?
  3. Improve a Prompt: The following prompt is too simple: "A car on a road."
    Rewrite this prompt to make it much more detailed and interesting by adding a **Style**, **Composition**, and **Lighting** component.
    Hint: What kind of car? What does the road look like? What time of day is it? Is it a photo or a drawing?
  4. Deconstruct a Prompt: Break down the complex prompt below into its four main components: Subject, Style, Lighting, and Composition.
    Prompt: "Digital art of a futuristic city skyline at twilight, cinematic lighting, wide angle shot."
    Hint: Go through the prompt phrase by phrase and match each part to one of the categories from the text.
  5. Creative Application: Imagine you want to create an image of a 'magical library'. Write a complete prompt for this idea, making sure to include at least four of the five components discussed in the text (Subject, Style, Composition, Lighting, Color Palette).
    Hint: Start with the subject. Then, decide if you want it to look like a realistic photo, a fantasy painting, or something else. How is the room lit? Are we looking at one bookshelf or the whole room?
  6. Troubleshooting: An AI keeps generating images of an apple with a computer logo on it. You just want a simple piece of fruit. What kind of specific subject description could you use to avoid the brand logo?
    Hint: The text emphasizes being specific. How can you describe the fruit in a way that is purely natural and organic?

Key Takeaways & Summary

Great job on completing the exercise! Here are the main points to remember:

  • Be Specific: Vague prompts lead to generic images. The more detail you provide, the closer the AI will get to your vision.
  • Layer Your Details: Start with a simple subject and add layers of detail like style, lighting, and composition.
  • Experiment: There's no single "correct" prompt. The best way to learn is to practice, mix and match components, and see what the AI creates. Think of it as a creative conversation.
